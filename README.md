
Full Project  (Times-series-Analysis-on-Stock-data-sets)


Project Title: Time Series Analysis and Forecasting of Bajaj Auto Stock Prices
Dataset: Daily stock prices of Bajaj Auto (2008–2019), focusing on 'Close' price.
Objective: Analyze historical stock price patterns and forecast future values.
Key Steps & Insights:

Loading & Plotting: Long-term upward trend with increasing volatility.
Decomposition: Strong trend, weak annual seasonality, random residuals.
Smoothing: Moving average (30-day) and exponential smoothing highlight trend.
SARIMA Modeling: (1,1,1)x(1,1,1,252) captures trend and weak seasonality.
Evaluation: RMSE ~200–300 INR (reasonable for price range ~500–3200).
Forecast: Predicts continued growth with volatility.

Conclusion: SARIMA effectively models stock price trend. Useful for investment insights, though stocks are inherently unpredictable.
